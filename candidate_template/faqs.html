Frequently Asked Questions

Jobnova is a platform that connects job seekers with employers, offering a wide range of job opportunities across various industries.

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, provide your details, and follow the instructions to complete your registration.

Once you have created an account, browse through the available jobs, select one that interests you, and click "Apply" to submit your application.

Yes, Jobnova is completely free for job seekers to use. Employers may have subscription plans for posting job listings.

You can update your profile by logging into your account, navigating to your profile page, and editing your information there.

Yes, you can apply for as many jobs as you like on Jobnova. Each application will be handled separately by employers.

Once you complete your profile and job preferences, Jobnova will suggest jobs based on your qualifications and interests.

Currently, feedback on applications is not provided automatically, but you can reach out to the employer directly for more information.
